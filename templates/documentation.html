Fraud Detection

Fraud detection (In a range of industries, including banking and insurance, fraud detection is used. Banking fraud also includes things like check forgery and the use of stolen credit cards). The purpose of fraud detection is to correctly identify legitimate and fraudulent credit card transactions, which is a data mining classification task. only a little research has been published in this field due to the lack of real-world data on which researchers may conduct experiments. The bank's unwillingness to provide sensitive consumer transaction data for privacy reasons. Shopping through internet-based applications and paying bills online has become commonplace, thus a physical card is no longer required to make transactions.
